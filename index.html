The base-process: choosing the wood

We carefully choose the pieces of wood that will be used for the products.

  • We observe the grain of the wood.
  • We analyse the veneers present on it.
  • We compare the colors to see of it is the right one.

Designing the product after the customer's needs and wishes

We especially design each product after hearing the customer wish.

  • We come up with a design, together with the customer.
  • We choose the colors and the model, so that it fits the place where the products is going to stay.
  • We make sure we choose the finest details for the products.

The process of creating the product

  • We treat the wood with special solution to make it last longer.
  • The wood is carefully cut, so that it will fit perfectly with the other pieces of wood.
  • We dry out the wood to make the final product.

Hand-crafted furniture for special homes.

We deliver the final product as soon as possible

Before giving the product to the customer, we make sure that all the details are placed and the finite product reaches our standards.

  • We do the final touches before the customer's arrival.
  • We check once more if the design is the same with the client's wish.
  • We prepare the product for the delivery.
